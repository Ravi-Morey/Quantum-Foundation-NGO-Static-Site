About Us


Quantum group. Is a dynamic organization that has been in the field of training for over 20 years. With its rich industry experience and expert faculty, Quantum has an edge in its field due to its consistent reputation of quality and adaptability.

Dr.Pratik Surana, founder and chief mentor at Quantum, has diligently dedicated his vast industry knowledge and guidance skills to make Quantum an unmatched leader in the corporate training realm. He has worked with some of the top organizations in India and has empowered employees across ages, cultures and hierarchy to achieve their life and productivity goals in the most effective manner.

As a part of its social initiative, Quantum has an ambition to make 1 million people Emotionally Literate by 2030.

Dr.Surana is committed to contribute to the community by making one million people aware of their emotional persona and its effects on their personal and professional lives.
